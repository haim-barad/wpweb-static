FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does WordPress maintenance include?

Our WordPress maintenance includes daily backups, security monitoring, core updates, plugin updates, performance optimization, uptime monitoring, and regular security scans to keep your site secure and running smoothly.

Do you support ecommerce websites?

Yes, we support ecommerce websites. This requires either the Pro or Premium plan due to the added complexity and higher uptime requirements of ecommerce sites running WooCommerce or similar.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

Basic plan users receive email support within 24 hours. Pro plan users get priority support with faster response times. Premium plan users have 24/7 phone support and a dedicated account manager.

What happens if my site gets hacked?

We provide malware removal and site recovery services. With our daily backups and security monitoring, we can quickly restore your site to a clean state and implement additional security measures to prevent future attacks.

What is GEO optimization and why does it matter?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website so AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity can discover, understand, and cite your content. Research shows sites with proper GEO optimization see 30–115% more visibility in AI-generated responses. As AI-powered search grows, GEO optimization is becoming as important as traditional SEO.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and we'll help you transition smoothly to ensure your site keeps running without interruption.
