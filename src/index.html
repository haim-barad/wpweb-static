Frequently Asked Questions

What does WordPress maintenance include?

Our WordPress maintenance includes daily backups, security monitoring, core updates, plugin updates, performance optimization, uptime monitoring, and regular security scans to keep your site secure and running smoothly.

Do you support ecommerce websites?

Yes, we support ecommerce websites and this requires either the Pro or Premium plan. In order to keep your site up 100% of the time and not miss any sales, you need the higher levels of support required for the added complexity of ecommerce websites.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

Basic plan users receive email support within 24 hours. Pro plan users get priority support with faster response times. Premium plan users have access to 24/7 phone support and dedicated account management.

What happens if my site gets hacked?

We provide malware removal and site recovery services. With our daily backups and security monitoring, we can quickly restore your site to a clean state and implement additional security measures to prevent future attacks.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and we'll help you transition smoothly between plans to ensure your site continues running without interruption.
